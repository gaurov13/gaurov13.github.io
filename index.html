Our Story


Our Mission: Do Good Actions for Our Community!

What Do We Do? Initially inspired by the medical professionals and nonprofit organizations that are providing critical services during the coronavirus crisis, Westhill High School rising senior Lorenzo Mazzara founded a fundraising group called Westhill Angels, together with his peers Nate Cooper, Talia Kunin, and Gaurov Bansal. The group includes more than twenty volunteers an 150 supporters from Westhill High School and the Stamford community. We are raising funds for healthcare workers at Stamford Hospital, providing meals for staff at CLC Children learning Center in Stamford among other good deeds for nonprofits and individuals in Fairfield County .Click on the following link to donate to our current cause. Support Our Fight Against Coronavirus!.


What is the coronavirus pandemic? COVID-19 is caused by a new coronavirus. Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses that are common in people and many different species of animals, including camels, cattle, cats, and bats. The toll that coronavirus is taking on everyday people is immense. The Centers of Disease Control has set out strict guidelines that should be followed. You can see the current death toll and other effects at this link: Information Tracker

Update: The fight against coronavirus is going very well, but we are not done yet and we will need a lot more of your support. Please continue to donate as we aspire to donate enough to Stamford Hopsital and other organizations around us. From all of us at Westhill Angels, thank you!
